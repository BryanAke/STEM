About Georgia STEM Incubator

It is imperative the Georgia produce more highly qualified STEM graduates and increase its STEM workforce development and STEM-based economic development. The State’s economy is dependent on a workforce with strong STEM knowledge and skills that can foster innovation and productivity. The National Governors Association has emphasized the importance of STEM because ‟the global economy has flattened the world in terms of skills and technology. A new workforce of problem-solvers, innovators, and inventors who are self-reliant and able to think logically is one of the critical foundations that drive a state economy’s innovation capacity.”
