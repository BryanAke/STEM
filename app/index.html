Why STEM?

Educating students in STEM subjects teaches them to think critically and solve problems — skills that can be used throughout life. Partnering with STEM programs within your local area ensures that your community thrives in the future.

About the Georgia STEM Incubator

The movement to advance STEM education in Georgia is gaining momentum, but it can still be difficult to find potential partners and quality resources. The Incubator's mission is to spur innovation in STEM education through reflective practice, professional learning, and collaboration.
