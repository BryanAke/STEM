Imagine a Truly Connected
STEM Network

The future of Georgia’s economy will be determined by the quality of education our children receive today. Individuals, schools, and businesses throughout the state are pushing the STEM movement forward and we’re seeing the positive ripples.

But what could we achieve if we worked together?

Join our Community

We’ve created a space for you to ask questions, share lessons or ideas, and meet other people who are interested in STEM education. You don’t have to be an educator to sign up. We are extremely interested in real world STEM applications from local businesses.
